The Day Everything Changed

There you were. Standing at Delhi Station. Waiting for me. My heart was already racing before I even stepped off the train.

I'd always adored you, Neha — from the very beginning. But I was terrified. Back in Bangalore, watching you get ready to leave for Canada... I wanted to say something, anything. But the words never came. And I let you go.

I thought I'd lost my chance forever.

But life had other plans. When I was coming to Delhi, there was this quiet hope inside me — maybe, just maybe, we could be something more. I wished with everything I had that you'd hold my heart this time... and not let it break.

And then — the auto ride. You sat next to me, and our hands found each other. No words needed. Just your fingers laced through mine, and suddenly the whole city disappeared.

We went to CP. We laughed. We talked like no time had passed at all. And somewhere between the lights and the noise and you looking at me with those eyes — I knew. I was done running from this.

So I braved it. I asked if I could kiss you.

And that kiss? That kiss sealed everything. In that one moment, every fear, every missed chance, every "what if" — they all melted away. It was just you and me.

I always knew — if you took one step towards me, I'd take two. And you didn't just take a step, Neha.

You ran straight into my heart. 💕
